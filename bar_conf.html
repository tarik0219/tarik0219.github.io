Adjusted Offense average: An estimate of the offensive efficiency (points scored per 100 possessions) of a conference against an average D1 school

Adjusted Defence: An estimate of the deffensive efficiency (points allowed per 100 possessions) of a conference against an average D1 school

Adjusted Offense - Adjusted Defence: An estimate of the overall efficiency (points difference per 100 possessions) of a conference against an average D1 school

Offensive Rebound %: Offensive Rebound Rate for a given possesion average for the conference

3PT FG%: Three-Point Shooting Percentage average for the conference

2PT FG%: Two-Point Shooting Percentage average for the conference
