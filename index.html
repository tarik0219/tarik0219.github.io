College Basketball 2019-2020 Top 6 Conferences Teams

Mouse over the data points to get more statistics for each team and to compare with other teams in the conference.

The six graphs represent each statitics described below comparison to win% and shows which ones corelate the most.

Win %: Teams overall win % for the given season.

Adjusted Offense average: An estimate of the offensive efficiency (points scored per 100 possessions) of a team against an average D1 school.

Adjusted Defence: An estimate of the deffensive efficiency (points allowed per 100 possessions) of a team against an average D1 school.

Adjusted Offense - Adjusted Defence: An estimate of the overall efficiency (points difference per 100 possessions) of a team against an average D1 school.

Offensive Rebound %: Offensive Rebound Rate for a given possesion average for the team.

3PT FG%: Three-Point Shooting Percentage average for the team.

2PT FG%: Two-Point Shooting Percentage average for the team.
